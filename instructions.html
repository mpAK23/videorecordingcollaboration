CollaborativeVideo Recording

Equipment Required

  • Desktop or Tablet with a Web Browser (Chrome recommended)
  • Phone with browser which serves as a camera and a person anywhere on the internet that has a device connected to the website for recording.
  • Headphones for monitoring, both locally and the remote.

You can record up to four videos with tools that aid in having them in sync. Note, nothing is saved until the export at the bottom, for the mp4, is pressed.

On a desktop computer or tablet click the "Create Control Center" button. Upon entry, at the top you will see a code. Open the same website on a phone and enter the code in the block on the page labeled "Camera Mode." From the control center click the + for the first block you want to record. You will see the view your phone has. It uses the rear camera.

Once ready to record, click the red record button. It will count down. Click to stop. You can play it back. You can delete it. When ready to add a second track, you can see the tick in the first track "Mon." Make sure that is ticked and with headsets listen to the first track as you play along. Again, it will count down.

To add a person not in the immediate vicinity, click the tick "Remote Mode," click "New Camera Session." That disconnects the current local camera from the control center and allows one to provide a remote user the new code. The code must be passed via phone, text, email, etc. Once connected to new performer can click "I'm Ready" and the control center can click the red dot to start their recording and countdown. So, the remote performer needs to be within four seconds of being ready and monitoring the sound on the phone.

Once the tracks are recorded there is also an Exp tick where you can decide if you want all recorded videos to be included in the export.

Finally, before hitting export, you can name the file.
